NexFlow GmbH is a Berlin-based B2B SaaS company founded in 2021. The company specializes in AI-powered marketing automation and intelligent lead qualification for mid-sized B2B enterprises across Germany and the DACH region.

Mission:
To help B2B companies convert more qualified leads through intelligent automation while reducing manual marketing workload by at least 40%.

Vision:
To become the leading AI-driven revenue enablement platform for European B2B companies by 2028.

Funding:
NexFlow raised €3.2 million in seed funding in 2022 from German and European venture capital firms.

Market Focus:
• Mid-sized B2B SaaS companies
• Industrial technology firms
• Manufacturing and logistics software providers
• Professional services companies

Growth Milestones:
• 150+ active customers across Germany, Austria, and Switzerland
• 38% average increase in qualified lead conversion across customers
• 92% customer retention rate
• 4.7/5 average customer satisfaction rating

Core Differentiator:
Unlike traditional CRMs, NexFlow combines AI-driven behavioral scoring with multi-channel campaign automation in one unified platform.

Compliance:
Fully GDPR-compliant, hosted on secure EU-based servers.
