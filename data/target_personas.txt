Target Personas for NexFlow

Persona 1 – Executive (CEO / COO / CFO)
Primary Goals:
• Increase revenue predictability
• Improve ROI on marketing spend
• Reduce operational inefficiencies

Pain Points:
• High customer acquisition costs
• Low conversion visibility
• Inconsistent lead quality

Language Preference:
• Data-driven
• Strategic
• ROI-focused
• Risk-aware

Avoid:
• Technical jargon
• Overly tactical marketing details

CTA Style:
• Strategic consultation
• ROI assessment call
• Executive demo

---

Persona 2 – Developer (CTO / Technical Lead)
Primary Goals:
• Ensure system reliability
• Evaluate integration feasibility
• Maintain data security standards

Pain Points:
• API limitations
• Security compliance risks
• Poor documentation

Language Preference:
• Technical
• Specific
• Architecture-focused

Avoid:
• Marketing buzzwords
• Vague performance claims

CTA Style:
• Technical demo
• API documentation review
• Integration walkthrough

---

Persona 3 – Marketing Manager (Head of Growth / Marketing)
Primary Goals:
• Increase qualified leads
• Launch campaigns faster
• Improve engagement metrics

Pain Points:
• Manual campaign work
• Low email open rates
• Poor segmentation

Language Preference:
• Energetic
• Results-oriented
• Tactical

Avoid:
• Overly financial framing
• Technical infrastructure details

CTA Style:
• Start free trial
• Launch campaign now
• Download templates
